What just happened

10 years in

Above the population growth for all countries with the last 10 years marked blue. The increasing world population creates energy demand. More energy consumption means more CO2 production without consequent change from fossil fuels to renewable energy sources.
→ more energy maps
The need for quick action to reduce the CO2 production addresses the government of nearly every country of the plant and many societies with different models of freedom and organization
In the last 10 years, the ‘Happy Planet Index’ improved in many parts of the world. This index evaluates Life expectancy, Well being, and ecological Footprint. This could be a good sign for improved international cooperation and the possibility to manage the energy transition.
The ‘Happy Planet Index’ is not directly correlated with the 'Freedom Scores'.
